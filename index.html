September 21, 2023

This article talks about the impacts and current trends of AI (artificial intellignce) technology on the world of photography and art. I have often thought that AI is bad for the world of photography and art in general. It felt fake and unauthentic to create and benefit from art or photos generated by AI. This article has changed my mind through arguing that AI will actually make us more efficient in our use of photography. Technologies like Arsenal can help newcomers into professional photography by recommending settings to create a good photo. Photo manipulation has always been a large part of the photography world, AI is simply another tool in our arsenal to enhance our photo abilities, and create new and exciting things. I think that AI can lead to making things like professional photography more accesible to small businesses and startups as well as enhancing the work of professionals. In the end, AI is not the death of traditional photography, but a new era for photographers to experiment with.

AI can monitor biodiversity through animal sounds


https://www.earth.com/news/ai-can-monitor-biodiversity-through-animal-sounds/
October 20, 2023

'Bioacoustics' can be used to track the biodiversity of an area and scientists are finding ways to incorporate AI in this tracking. Reforested areas, expecially in tropical forests, are critical for the future of biodiversity and climate change on our planet. Using AI to detect different sounds, different species can be identified and tracked in an area that was lacking in biodiversity to see how that changes over time.
